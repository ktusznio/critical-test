How does it work?

Start with your JUULpod

Remove colored cap & insert cartridge end into device. Voila - it becomes your mouthpiece as well as the flavorful engine that powers JUUL.

No buttons or switches, just draw to get it going - carefully at first, it may seem strong to first-timers.

Consult the Indicator

The indicator glows during use to reflect pull strength and while charging. When pod is inserted, gently tap device twice to show charge level.

  • green = high
  • yellow = medium
  • red = low
Charge it

Stand device in magnetic USB charger. Reaches full charge in 1 hour.
